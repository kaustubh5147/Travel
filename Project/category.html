Sanitized Environment Buses to be deep-cleaned and disinfected after every trip. All buses to be equipped with hand sanitizers for staff and passengers. Safe Practices All passengers to mandatorily wear masks at all times. Any passenger without a mask will be denied entry to the bus. Pillows, bedsheets & blankets to be made unavailable as a precautionary measure. Passengers to carry their personal linen, if required.
